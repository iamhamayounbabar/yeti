Epic - Cloud Nine Farms

Situated on a cloud high above the mountain ranges, the Cloud Nine Farm features a spacious Yeti Hut designed to accommodate 4 Yeti workers, a power source, and a mining rig.

Residing at the summit of the unclimbable Cloud Peak, Cloud Yeti are able to maintain their secrecy, far from the reach of curious onlookers, while they conquer the clouds. As an epic farm type, only 400 of these farms will ever exist. The unique location of the Cloud Nine Farm promotes an exceptional synergy with the enigmatic Cloud Yeti, resulting in a 10% farming boost when upgraded level 10.

Nestled within a pristine glacier on Ice Peak, the Arctic Glacier Farm features a comfortable Yeti Hut built to house 3 Yeti workers, a power source, and a mining rig,
